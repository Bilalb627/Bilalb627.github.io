GLO-BUS Simulation | Global Business Management

Description:

As part of a collaborative group project for the worldwide Business Management course, I participated in the GLO-BUS Simulation, which simulates the experience of operating a virtual firm in a competitive worldwide market. Collaborated closely with coworkers to make strategic decisions across several company departments, acquiring practical expertise in business management and strategy creation.

Key Contributions:

  • Team Strategy Development: Collaborated with team members to create and revise our company's strategy plan, considering market dynamics, competitive positioning, and resource restrictions. During team talks, I contributed ideas and insights that helped develop our overall approach.

  • Market Analysis and Research: Conducted market research to better understand customer preferences, market trends, and the competitive landscape. We used market data and industry studies to influence our product development and marketing plans, ensuring that they were in line with market demands.

  • Financial Planning and Analysis: Assist with budgeting, forecasting, and financial performance review. Collaborated with team members to examine financial statements and key performance indicators, finding opportunities for improvement and optimization.

  • Operations Management: Helped optimize operational procedures and resource allocation to increase efficiency and production. Worked together with team members to execute operational strategies that were consistent with our company's aims and objectives.

  • Effective Communication and Collaboration: Throughout the simulation, I kept open lines of contact with my teammates, giving updates, thoughts, and concerns. Actively engaged in team meetings and discussions to create a friendly and collaborative atmosphere.

Outcome:

Participating in the GLO-BUS Simulation was a fantastic learning opportunity for global company management and strategy execution in a team context. Our team successfully navigated hurdles, capitalized on opportunities, and achieved competitive success in the virtual marketplace via effective cooperation and strategic decision-making, exhibiting business acumen and teamwork competency.
